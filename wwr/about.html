River Wranglers

At River Wranglers, we have "Deep Roots and Wild Waters." We make it our mission to ensure everyone has the best experience on their trip. Whether you are locals or from out of town, you will want to stop by and meet the family! We have skilled guides who know how to make sure everyone has a good time while staying safe.

History

This company is completely made up of one family from a local farm here in Utah. Growing up, the owner, Jack, would often come to the rivers with his cousins to tame the waters. With this grew a passion for water rafting and his wife, Kim, shares the same drive. Jack, Kim, their kids, and his extended family operate River Wranglers and have for over 25 years. He wants to share this love with everyone he meets.

In addition to riding the rapids, his family provides a lot of local produce that is sold at the farmer's market every weekend during the summer and fall. Don't forget to stop by, meet the owners, and enjoy some of the best produce around!
